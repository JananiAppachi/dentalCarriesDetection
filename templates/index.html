About Your Teeth

Teeth are vital for chewing, speaking, and maintaining overall oral health. Each tooth has an enamel, dentin, pulp, and root. Maintaining healthy teeth helps prevent cavities, caries, and other dental issues.

Oral Hygiene Tips

Proper hygiene prevents cavities, caries, and bad breath. Follow these tips:

Brush Twice Daily

Use fluoride toothpaste and brush for at least 2 minutes.

Floss Regularly

Clean between teeth to remove hidden food particles.

Mouthwash

Use antiseptic mouthwash to reduce bacteria and freshen breath.

Nutrition for Strong Teeth

Strong teeth require calcium, phosphorus, and vitamins. These nutrients come from your daily diet.

Milk & Dairy

Rich in calcium and vitamin D for stronger enamel and bones.

Cheese

Helps neutralize acid and strengthens teeth.

Leafy Greens

Spinach, kale, and broccoli provide calcium and vitamins for oral health.
